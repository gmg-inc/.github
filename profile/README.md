# Global Momentum Group

![GMG logo](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/gmg-inc/.github/refs/heads/main/profile/gmg.png)

We build, launch, and operate ventures.

GMG is a holding company founded by three partners with backgrounds in software engineering, infrastructure, and business operations. We back our own projects across different industries, from early concept to production.

## How We Work

We move first and refine later. Every venture under GMG starts with a bias toward action: validate fast, build lean, iterate in public when possible. We keep teams small, decisions fast, and overhead low.

## Open Source

Some of our work ends up here. Internal tools, libraries, and infrastructure components that we think are useful beyond our own walls. If it's public, it's maintained. We don't publish abandonware.
